The range is approximately $170--$250/yr, which aligns with the claim when rounded. Google review count was not verified from these pages (would require a separate Google Maps lookup).

- Source: https://americasmailbox.com/rates-and-plans

**Claim 3: Escapees RV Club pricing -- $110--$150/yr (mail) + $50 enrollment + $50 postage deposit; $49.95/yr club membership.**

- Verdict: CONFIRMED
- Source: https://escapeesmailservice.com, https://escapeesmailservice.com/escapees-mail-service-sign-up/, https://escapees.com/join/

**Claim 4: DakotaPost pricing -- $155/mo (monthly forwarding), $210/mo (weekly).**
- Paper says: $155/mo (monthly forwarding), $210/mo (weekly forwarding)
- Found: DakotaPost offers two plans: My DakotaMail Premium at $199/yr (+ $25 setup fee) and My DakotaMail VIP at $259/yr (+ $25 setup fee). These are **annual** prices, not monthly. The Premium plan allows choosing forwarding frequency (1x/month, 2x/month, 1x/week). There is no $155/mo or $210/mo pricing anywhere on the site. The paper's figures appear to be either fabricated or based on outdated/incorrect information.
- Verdict: CONTRADICTED
- Source: https://www.dakotapost.net/services/mail-forwarding, https://www.dakotapost.net/sign-up

**Claim 5: Nomad Tax -- Individual returns from $450; FEIE +$200; merging into Basta+Croop.**

The base price of $565 contradicts the claimed $450 starting price. It is possible that the $450 figure was accurate for Nomad Tax before the merger, but cannot be verified as the old site no longer exists.
