## Full Findings

**Claim 1: TaxDay pricing -- $9.99/mo with 30-day free trial, GPS tracking, all 50 state tax rules**
- Paper says: $9.99/mo, 30-day free trial, GPS tracking, all 50 state tax rules
- Found: $9.99/mo confirmed on both website and App Store. The website states "first 30 days free," but the App Store listing says "risk-free 45-day trial." GPS tracking via mobile phone is confirmed. Coverage of "the complete set of tax rules for all 50 U.S. states" is confirmed on the website.
- Verdict: PARTIALLY CONFIRMED (trial length is 30 days per website but 45 days per App Store; all other details match)
- Source: https://www.taxday.com/ and https://apps.apple.com/us/app/taxday/id1045026503

**Claim 2: TaxBird pricing -- $4.99/mo or $39.99/yr with 30-day free trial, US state + country tracking**
- Paper says: $4.99/mo or $39.99/yr, 30-day free trial, US state + country tracking
- Found: $4.99/mo and $39.99/yr confirmed. 30-day free trial confirmed. The app supports country-level tracking worldwide, state-level tracking in the US, and province-level tracking in Canada.
- Verdict: CONFIRMED
- Source: https://www.taxbird.com/ and https://apps.apple.com/us/app/taxbird-residency-tracker/id1238166926

**Claim 3: Domicile365 -- free (60-day trial), paid pricing not disclosed, 15-min GPS intervals**
- Paper says: Free with 60-day trial; paid pricing not disclosed; 15-minute GPS intervals
- Found: 60-day free trial confirmed (no credit card required). However, paid pricing IS publicly disclosed: $19.99/mo for an individual subscription, with annual plans available. 15-minute interval GPS tracking is confirmed on the website and FAQ.
- Verdict: PARTIALLY CONFIRMED (60-day trial and 15-min GPS intervals confirmed; but paid pricing is publicly available at $19.99/mo, contradicting "not disclosed")
- Source: https://www.domicile365.com/pricing.html

**Claim 4: Flamingo Compliance -- ~$30/yr (basic), auto movement recording**
- Paper says: ~$30/yr for basic plan, automatic movement recording
- Found: The basic "Settler" tier is $29.99/yr ($2.99/mo), which matches "~$30/yr." The app uses geolocation to automatically record location and create a travel timeline. The domain flamingotracker.com now redirects to flamingo.tax. All new users get 30 days of full-feature access before choosing a tier.
- Verdict: CONFIRMED
- Source: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/flamingo-tax-visa-tracker/id1631680437 and https://flamingo.tax/
